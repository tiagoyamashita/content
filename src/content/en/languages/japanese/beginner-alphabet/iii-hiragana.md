## Grammar drill — read aloud

These endings appear in every ticket and email:

| Word | Reading | English |
|------|---------|---------|
| します | shi-ma-su | do (polite) |
| しました | shi-ma-shi-ta | did (polite past) |
| してください | shi-te-ku-da-sai | please do |
| できません | de-ki-ma-sen | cannot |
| です | de-su | is / am (polite) |
| ません | ma-sen | not (polite negative) |
| 設定 | se-tte-i | settings |

## Tips

1. Hiragana = **native Japanese** + **grammar** — pair with kanji in 設定 (せってい).
2. **は** reads **wa** as the topic particle; **he** as in へや (he-ya — room).
3. Kanji often have hiragana okurigana after them — read kanji first, then trailing hiragana.
