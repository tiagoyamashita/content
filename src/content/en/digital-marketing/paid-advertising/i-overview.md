Paid advertising — overview
**Paid advertising** buys **reach and clicks** on demand — search, social, and display. It accelerates what already works; it rarely fixes a broken offer or untracked funnel.

Run [Analytics](../analytics/i-overview.md) and [Conversions](../analytics/iv-conversions-and-attribution.md) before scaling spend.

## 1. When paid helps

| Good fit | Poor fit |
|----------|----------|
| Landing page converts organically | No conversion tracking |
| Clear ICP and offer | Product-market fit unknown |
| Need leads/sales **now** | Expect paid to replace product work |
| Unit economics modeled | CAC unknown |

## 2. Channel choice (quick)

| Channel | Intent | Typical use |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Google Search** | High — active search | Capture "buy X" / "best Y" |
| **Meta (FB/IG)** | Mixed — interrupt | Awareness, retargeting, B2C |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B targeting | Job title, company size |
| **Display / YouTube** | Lower direct intent | Brand, remarketing |

Match [funnel stage](../foundations/ii-channels-and-funnel.md) — search for bottom funnel; social for top/mid with strong creative.

## 3. Paid vs organic (mindset)

```text
Organic / SEO  →  asset (ranks for months)
Paid           →  throttle (stops when budget stops)
```

Use paid to **test messaging** and **feed retargeting pools** while SEO compounds.

## 4. Pre-launch checklist

```text
[ ] Conversion events fire in GA4
[ ] UTM template documented
[ ] Landing page matches ad promise
[ ] Budget cap and CPA/ROAS target written down
[ ] Kill rule: pause if CPA > X for Y days
```
