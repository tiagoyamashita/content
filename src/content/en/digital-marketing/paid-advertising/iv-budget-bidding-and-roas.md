Budget, bidding & ROAS
Paid fails without **budget discipline** and clear **unit economics**. Define CPA/ROAS targets and kill rules before increasing spend.

## 1. Core formulas

| Metric | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| **CPC** | Spend ÷ clicks |
| **CPA** | Spend ÷ conversions |
| **ROAS** | Revenue ÷ ad spend |
| **CTR** | Clicks ÷ impressions |
| **CVR** | Conversions ÷ clicks |

Example: $500 spend, 25 conversions → CPA = $20.

## 2. Set targets from business math

```text
LTV or gross margin per customer
  →  max affordable CAC (often fraction of LTV)
  →  max CPA for lead if lead-to-customer rate known
```

| If… | Then… |
|-----|-------|
| LTV = $600, target CAC ≤ $200 | Pause keywords with CPA > $200 after enough data |
| 10% trial → paid, ARPU $50/mo | Model trial CPA, not just signup |

From [Goals & metrics](../foundations/iv-goals-and-metrics.md).

## 3. Learning phase and sample size

| Mistake | Reality |
|---------|---------|
| Judging after $20 spend | Too few conversions for signal |
| Changing everything daily | Resets learning |

Wait for **~30–50 conversions** per major test (rule of thumb) before big structural changes.

## 4. Budget allocation

| Stage | Allocation |
|-------|------------|
| **Test** | Small daily caps; 2–3 campaigns max |
| **Validate** | Double budget on CPA-positive campaigns only |
| **Scale** | Increase 15–30% every few days — not 10× overnight |

Keep **10–20%** budget for experiments always.

## 5. Kill rules (write these down)

```text
Pause ad group if CPA > $X for 7 days and spend > $Y
Pause keyword if 0 conversions and spend > $Z
Pause creative if CTR < benchmark after 1k impressions
```

Automate pauses in-platform where possible.

## 6. Scaling channels

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Search exact keywords profitable | Add phrase, SKAG expansion carefully |
| Meta retargeting ROAS strong | Increase budget; test lookalikes |
| LinkedIn CPL acceptable | Expand titles slowly — watch frequency |

**Diminishing returns** appear — monitor CPA weekly as spend rises.

## 7. Seasonality and external shocks

| Event | Response |
|-------|----------|
| Competitor sale | Don't panic-bid; improve offer/creative |
| Q4 B2C | CPC rises — adjust targets |
| Tracking break | Pause until fixed — flying blind wastes money |
