Social & display
**Social ads** (Meta, LinkedIn, X, TikTok) and **display** reach users who may not be searching yet. Creative and audience targeting matter more than keywords.

## 1. Platform fit

| Platform | Strength | Weak for |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Meta (FB/IG)** | B2C, broad demographics, retargeting | Niche B2B with tiny audience |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B job title / company | Low budgets (high CPC) |
| **TikTok** | Short video, younger demos | Complex B2B explainers |
| **Google Display / YouTube** | Remarketing, awareness | Cold conversion without creative |

## 2. Campaign objectives map to funnel

| Objective | Funnel | Note |
|-----------|--------|------|
| **Awareness / reach** | Top | Cheap impressions; track view-through carefully |
| **Traffic** | Mid | Optimize for landing page views |
| **Leads / conversions** | Bottom | Needs pixel + conversion API |
| **Retargeting** | Mid–bottom | Site visitors, video viewers, email list upload |

## 3. Audience types

| Audience | Use |
|----------|-----|
| **Core / interest** | Cold — test ICP hypotheses |
| **Lookalike** | Similar to customers or email list |
| **Custom (retargeting)** | Warm — visited site, engaged with content |
| **Uploaded list** | CRM match — account-based |

Start **retargeting** before broad cold spend if traffic exists.

## 4. Creative that works

```text
Hook in first 3 seconds (video) or first line (static)
One clear message — not feature dump
Social proof when possible
Obvious CTA
Native aspect ratio per platform
```

| Test | Variants |
|------|----------|
| Hook | Problem vs outcome vs question |
| Format | Static vs short video vs carousel |
| CTA | "Start trial" vs "See demo" |

Refresh creative every 2–4 weeks — fatigue drops performance.

## 5. LinkedIn B2B notes

| Tactic | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Narrow targeting | Job function + seniority + company size |
| Lead gen forms | In-platform forms — higher CPL, lower friction |
| Thought leader ads | Boost founder posts — often outperforms corporate |

Expect **higher CPC** than Meta — justify with deal size.

## 6. Display and remarketing

| Type | Role |
|------|------|
| **Remarketing banners** | Remind site visitors |
| **YouTube in-stream** | Skippable video to warm audiences |
| **Performance Max** | Google automates mix — monitor search terms |

Cap frequency so users don't feel stalked.
