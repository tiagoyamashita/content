Channels & funnel
Customers rarely buy on first visit. The **funnel** describes stages from stranger to buyer; **channels** are how you reach people at each stage.

## 1. Classic funnel stages

```text
Awareness     →  they learn you exist
Consideration →  they compare options
Conversion    →  they buy or sign up
Retention     →  they stay, expand, refer
```

| Stage | Job | Example content |
|-------|-----|-----------------|
| **Awareness** | Educate, entertain, show up in search | Blog posts, social, PR |
| **Consideration** | Compare, prove fit | Case studies, demos, reviews |
| **Conversion** | Remove friction | Pricing, signup, checkout |
| **Retention** | Deliver value, reduce churn | Onboarding email, help docs |

Not every business needs all four equally — B2B SaaS often has a long consideration phase; impulse B2C may skip straight to conversion ads.

## 2. Channel map

| Channel | Best stage | Speed | Compounds? |
|---------|------------|-------|------------|
| **Organic search (SEO)** | Awareness, consideration | Slow | Yes |
| **Content / blog** | Awareness, consideration | Medium | Yes |
| **Email** | Consideration, conversion, retention | Fast once list exists | Yes |
| **Paid search** | Conversion (high intent) | Instant | No |
| **Paid social** | Awareness, consideration | Instant | No |
| **Organic social** | Awareness, community | Medium | Sometimes |
| **Referral / partners** | All stages | Depends | Depends |
| **Direct / brand** | Conversion | — | Yes (brand equity) |

## 3. Pick a primary channel (early stage)

You cannot excel at every channel with a tiny team. Choose **one primary** and **one secondary**:

| If your buyer… | Primary | Secondary |
|----------------|---------|-----------|
| Googles problems before buying | SEO + content | Email capture |
| Discovers on LinkedIn / X | Organic social | Paid retargeting later |
| Needs trust from peers | Case studies + email | Partner referrals |
| Searches "buy X near me" | Local SEO + Google Business Profile | Local ads |

Revisit quarterly — what sends **qualified** traffic and conversions, not just vanity metrics.

## 4. Full-funnel example (B2B SaaS)

```text
Awareness:     "What is X?" blog post ranks on Google
Consideration: Comparison page + webinar signup (email)
Conversion:    Free trial from email nurture
Retention:     Onboarding sequence + product emails
```

Each asset should link to the **next logical step** (internal links + CTAs).

## 5. Channel–metric cheat sheet

| Channel | Watch |
|---------|-------|
| SEO | Impressions, clicks, rankings for target queries |
| Content | Time on page, scroll depth, assisted conversions |
| Email | Open rate (directional), click rate, revenue per send |
| Paid | CPC, CPA, ROAS, landing page conversion rate |
| Social | Profile visits, link clicks, saves/shares (platform-dependent) |

See [Goals & metrics](iv-goals-and-metrics.md) for how these roll up.

## 6. Common mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Only bottom-funnel ads, no awareness content | Publish top-funnel content or pay for reach |
| Same message on every channel | Adapt format — LinkedIn ≠ TikTok ≠ search snippet |
| Measuring only top of funnel | Track signup or purchase, not just traffic |
| Abandoning SEO after 8 weeks | Organic takes months — commit or don't start |
