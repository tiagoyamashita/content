**Foundations** covers the vocabulary and mental models every other digital marketing note builds on — who you serve, which channels exist, and how success is measured.

## 1. What digital marketing is

Digital marketing is **planned communication** through online channels to attract, convert, and retain customers. It is not:

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| "Post everywhere and hope" | Pick channels that match your buyer and stage |
| "SEO tricks without a product" | Traffic to a weak offer still fails |
| "Set ads and forget" | Paid needs landing pages, tracking, and kill rules |

## 2. The growth loop

```text
Understand audience  →  clear offer & message
  →  publish useful content / pages
  →  distribute (search, email, social, paid, partners)
  →  measure (traffic, signups, revenue)
  →  iterate
```

Each later submenu in this track maps to one step: [On-page SEO](../on-page-seo/i-overview.md), [Content strategy](../content-strategy/i-overview.md), [Link building](../link-building/i-overview.md), [Analytics](../analytics/i-overview.md), [Email](../email-marketing/i-overview.md), [Paid](../paid-advertising/i-overview.md).

## 3. Owned vs earned vs paid

| Type | You control | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Owned** | Full (mostly) | Website, email list, app |
| **Earned** | Influence only | Press, backlinks, word of mouth |
| **Paid** | Budget + creative | Google Ads, Meta ads, sponsorships |

**Compounding** channels (SEO, content, email) build assets. **Paid** buys reach on demand — useful after you know what converts.
