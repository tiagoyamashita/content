Editorial calendar & workflow
An **editorial calendar** turns strategy into shipped pages — with briefs, owners, and review so quality stays consistent.

## 1. Calendar granularity

| Horizon | Plan |
|---------|------|
| **Quarter** | Pillars, flagship assets, campaigns |
| **Month** | Spokes, updates, distribution beats |
| **Week** | Draft, review, publish slots |

Use a spreadsheet, Notion, Linear, or CMS calendar — tool matters less than **visible dates and owners**.

## 2. Content brief template

Every piece starts with a brief:

```text
Title (working)
Primary query + intent
ICP / funnel stage
Outline (H2/H3)
Unique angle / data sources
Internal links (pillar, money pages)
CTA
Owner + due dates (draft, review, publish)
Success metric (rank, signups, links)
```

Briefs prevent "start writing and see what happens."

## 3. Workflow stages

```text
Ideation  →  brief approved  →  draft  →  review  →  SEO check  →  publish  →  distribute
```

| Stage | Owner | Output |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **Ideation** | Marketing + product | Backlog ranked by impact |
| **Draft** | Writer | Google Doc / MD in repo |
| **Review** | Subject expert | Factual accuracy |
| **SEO check** | SEO or writer | Title, meta, links, intent — [On-page SEO](../on-page-seo/i-overview.md) |
| **Publish** | Whoever ships | Live URL, sitemap |
| **Distribute** | Marketing | Email, social, outreach list |

## 4. Cadence examples

| Team size | Realistic cadence |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Solo | 1 substantial piece / month + micro-updates |
| 2–3 people | 2–4 pieces / month + quarterly pillar refresh |
| Dedicated content | Weekly + ongoing updates |

**Update existing** high-traffic posts before chasing new URLs — often higher ROI.

## 5. Content governance

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| Style guide (voice, terms) | Consistent brand |
| Fact-check YMYL | Trust and compliance |
| Version history in git or CMS | Audit trail |
| Retirement policy | Merge or 301 outdated posts |

## 6. Meeting rhythm (lightweight)

| Meeting | Frequency | Agenda |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| **Content standup** | Weekly 15 min | Blockers, publishes this week |
| **Performance review** | Monthly | Top/bottom URLs, update queue |
| **Quarterly planning** | Quarterly | Pillars, assets, kill list |

Pull metrics from [Analytics](../analytics/i-overview.md).
