Distribution & repurposing
Publishing without **distribution** is hiding content in a closet. Repurpose once; distribute across owned channels — without harming SEO.

## 1. Distribution stack

```text
Publish on site (canonical)
  →  email to list
  →  social snippets (native posts, not only links)
  →  community (where allowed — no spam)
  →  outreach for link-worthy assets
  →  paid boost (optional, if tracked)
```

## 2. Owned channel playbook

| Channel | Tactic |
|---------|--------|
| **Email** | New post summary + one insight; link to full article — [Email marketing](../email-marketing/i-overview.md) |
| **Newsletter** | Curate best of month + single deep dive |
| **Social** | Thread: 3–5 tips from guide; link in last post |
| **Product** | In-app tip linking to help doc |
| **Sales** | Enablement: send case study to prospects |

## 3. Repurposing matrix

| Source asset | Derivatives |
|--------------|-------------|
| Long guide | LinkedIn carousel, YouTube walkthrough, podcast script |
| Webinar | Blog recap, clip shorts, slide deck on site |
| Survey data | Press pitch, infographic, X posts with one stat each |
| Case study | Quote graphics, sales one-pager |

One **canonical URL** on your site; derivatives drive traffic back.

## 4. Syndication rules (avoid duplicate SEO)

| Approach | SEO note |
|----------|----------|
| **Syndicate with canonical** | Partner uses `rel=canonical` to your original |
| **Publish excerpt + link** | 2–3 paragraphs max on Medium/LinkedIn |
| **Full duplicate on third party** | Avoid — competes with your URL |

When in doubt, publish summary off-site and link to full piece.

## 5. Timing and frequency

| Practice | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Email within 48h of publish** | List is warmest audience |
| **Social staggered** | Same day + reminder 1 week later |
| **Evergreen reshare** | Quarterly for pillars |
| **Launch coordination** | Product + blog + email same day for major releases |

## 6. Measure distribution

| Channel | Metric |
|---------|--------|
| Email | Click rate to URL |
| Social | Link clicks (UTM tagged) |
| Outreach | Links earned, replies |
| Paid boost | CPA vs organic baseline |

Use consistent [UTM parameters](../analytics/iv-conversions-and-attribution.md).
