Reporting & experiments
**Reporting** turns data into decisions. **Experiments** (A/B tests) validate changes before you bet the funnel on opinion.

## 1. One-page marketing dashboard

| Widget | Source |
|--------|--------|
| Organic clicks (28d) | Search Console |
| Sessions by channel | GA4 acquisition |
| Primary conversions | GA4 conversions |
| Conversion rate (landing) | GA4 |
| Top 5 landing pages | GA4 |
| Email click rate (last send) | ESP |

Refresh weekly; share async — no meeting required for basics.

## 2. Monthly narrative (template)

```text
What moved: [metric + delta]
Why we think: [hypothesis]
What we did: [ships]
Next month: [experiments + content]
```

Numbers without narrative invite wrong conclusions.

## 3. Cohort thinking

| Cohort | Question |
|--------|----------|
| Signups by month | Do newer cohorts activate better? |
| Traffic by content publish date | Which posts still earn traffic 6 months later? |
| Channel by first touch | Which source brings higher-LTV users? |

Compounding channels show **long tails** in cohort views.

## 4. Simple A/B tests

| Element | Test |
|---------|------|
| Headline | Value prop A vs B |
| CTA copy | "Start free trial" vs "Try free for 14 days" |
| Form length | Email only vs email + company |

**Rules:**

```text
One change at a time
Enough traffic (rough: hundreds of conversions per variant for small lifts)
Run full business weeks (avoid weekday skew)
Predefine success metric
```

Tools: VWO, Optimizely, Google Optimize successors, or built-in CMS experiments.

## 5. When not to A/B test

| Situation | Instead |
|-----------|---------|
| Low traffic | Qualitative user tests, bigger bold changes |
| Broken analytics | Fix tracking first |
| No hypothesis | User research before testing button color |

## 6. SEO experiments (careful)

Google does not offer clean "SEO A/B" in GA. Safer approaches:

| Approach | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Before/after** | Major rewrite — compare GSC clicks 8 weeks before/after |
| **Split URLs** | Test new template on subset of similar pages |
| **Holdback** | Don't change control group pages |

Avoid cloaking or showing different content to Google vs users.
