Analytics & measurement — overview
**Analytics** answers whether marketing work moves the business — not just whether traffic went up. Set up **Search Console + GA4 + conversions** before scaling spend or outreach.

## 1. Minimum viable measurement

```text
Google Search Console  →  search visibility
GA4 (or privacy-friendly alt)  →  traffic & behavior
1–3 conversion events  →  signup, purchase, demo
UTM discipline on campaigns  →  source attribution
```

Setup pointers: [Monitoring & analytics](../../startups/free-services/v-monitoring-analytics-and-devtools.md).

## 2. Questions analytics should answer

| Question | Tool / report |
|----------|---------------|
| Are we getting impressions for target queries? | Search Console |
| Which pages drive signups? | GA4 landing page + conversion |
| Did last month's blog post help? | Compare assisted conversions / path |
| Is paid cheaper than organic CAC? | UTMs + CRM or revenue data |
| Are links correlating with traffic? | GSC links + referral report |

## 3. Leading vs lagging (recap)

| Leading (weekly) | Lagging (monthly+) |
|------------------|---------------------|
| Impressions, clicks | Revenue, LTV |
| Trial starts | Retention |
| Email click rate | CAC payback |

From [Goals & metrics](../foundations/iv-goals-and-metrics.md).

## 4. Privacy and consent

| Region / context | Practice |
|------------------|----------|
| EU / UK | Consent banner before non-essential cookies where required |
| B2B low traffic | Often simpler; still document data flows |
| Server-side tracking | Reduces browser blocking — see [Paid attribution](../paid-advertising/v-attribution-and-privacy.md) |

Not legal advice — align with your counsel for GDPR/CCPA.
