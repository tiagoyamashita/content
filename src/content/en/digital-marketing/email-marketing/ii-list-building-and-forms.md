List building & forms
A list built on **permission** outperforms purchased lists. Grow subscribers with clear value, simple forms, and honest expectations.

## 1. Opt-in fundamentals

| Principle | Practice |
|-----------|----------|
| **Explicit consent** | "Subscribe to our newsletter" — not pre-checked boxes |
| **Clear value** | What will they receive and how often? |
| **Easy unsubscribe** | One click in every marketing email |
| **No bought lists** | Violates ESP ToS; destroys deliverability |

## 2. Lead magnets

Offer something useful for an email:

| Magnet type | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Checklist / template** | Security audit PDF |
| **Mini-course** | 5-day email series |
| **Tool access** | Calculator, Notion duplicate |
| **Exclusive content** | Report summary |

Magnet should match [ICP](../foundations/iii-audience-and-positioning.md) — generic "10 tips" converts poorly.

## 3. Form placement

| Location | Conversion note |
|----------|-----------------|
| **End of blog post** | High intent — they read your content |
| **Dedicated landing page** | Paid or social traffic |
| **Footer sitewide** | Low per-view rate, steady volume |
| **Exit intent popup** | Test — can annoy; use sparingly |

**Fields:** email only for top of funnel; add name/company when qualifying leads.

## 4. Single vs double opt-in

| Model | Pros | Cons |
|-------|------|------|
| **Single opt-in** | Faster growth | Typos, spam signups |
| **Double opt-in** | Cleaner list, proof of consent (GDPR-friendly) | Friction |

EU-focused B2C often prefers double opt-in; B2B SaaS often uses single with verification email on account creation.

## 5. Form copy template

```text
Headline: Get [specific outcome]
Sub: Weekly tips on [topic]. Unsubscribe anytime.
Button: Send me the [magnet / updates]
Privacy: We don't sell your email. [Privacy policy link]
```

## 6. List hygiene

| Practice | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Remove hard bounces | ISPs penalize repeat sends to dead addresses |
| Sunset inactive (6–12 mo) | Re-engagement campaign then remove |
| Segment by source | Compare magnet vs footer quality |
