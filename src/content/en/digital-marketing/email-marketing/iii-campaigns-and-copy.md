Campaigns & copy
**Campaigns** are one-off or recurring sends; **copy** determines opens (subject) and actions (body). Write for scanners — mobile-first, one primary CTA.

## 1. Campaign types

| Type | Cadence | Goal |
|------|---------|------|
| **Newsletter** | Weekly / monthly | Trust, traffic to content |
| **Launch** | Event-driven | Feature or product release |
| **Promotional** | Rare | Sale, discount — use sparingly |
| **Digest** | Automated | "Best of" curation |

Balance **value** vs **ask** — roughly 80/20 value to promotion for nurture lists.

## 2. Subject lines

| Tactic | Example |
|--------|---------|
| **Specific benefit** | "How we cut onboarding drop 22%" |
| **Curiosity (earned)** | "The metric we stopped tracking" |
| **Personal / segment** | "[Role] guide to X" |

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| ALL CAPS / !!! | Spam filters |
| Misleading clickbait | Unsubs and complaints |
| "Newsletter #47" | No reason to open |

Preview text matters — extend the subject, don't repeat it.

## 3. Body structure

```text
Hook (1–2 sentences)
Value (bullets or short sections)
One primary CTA (button)
Optional PS (secondary link or human note)
```

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| One goal per email | Five competing CTAs |
| Plain language | Wall of text |
| Link with descriptive text | "Click here" everywhere |

## 4. Segmentation basics

| Segment | Send |
|---------|------|
| **New subscribers** | Welcome series — [Automation](iv-automation-and-lifecycle.md) |
| **Active customers** | Advanced tips, upsell |
| **Cold leads** | Case studies, re-engagement |
| **By interest** | Tag from signup form or click behavior |

Even **two segments** beat blast-to-all.

## 5. Launch email sequence (example)

| Day | Email |
|-----|-------|
| 0 | Announcement + benefit + CTA |
| 2 | Social proof / demo |
| 5 | FAQ + objection handling |
| 7 | Last chance / reminder (if time-bound) |

Coordinate with [Content](../content-strategy/iv-editorial-calendar-and-workflow.md) and product release.

## 6. Measure and iterate

| Metric | Action if low |
|--------|---------------|
| Low clicks | Stronger CTA, fewer links, better segment fit |
| High unsubs | Too frequent or too promotional |
| High spam rate | Stop sends; audit list source and copy |

Tag links with [UTMs](../analytics/iv-conversions-and-attribution.md) for GA4.
