## How to use these notes

1. Read **day-to-day** — does the work energize you?
2. Check **Japan notes** — language and employer fit.
3. Follow **study path** into CS101 / SWE101 / SRE101 / AI101.
4. Recheck **comp** against [Compensation](../iii-compensation.md) when you interview.
