Garlic herb butter
Soft compound butter for warm [baguettes](../baking/iii-baguettes.md), steaks, vegetables, or melted over boiled potatoes. Make a log once and slice as needed. **All weights are in grams**.

**Yield:** about 130 g (enough for 1–2 baguettes) · **Total:** ~10 min + chill

## Equipment

- Kitchen scale (grams)
- Small bowl, fork or spatula
- Parchment or plastic wrap

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Weight (g) |
|------------|------------|
| Unsalted butter, softened | 100 |
| Garlic, finely grated or minced | 8 |
| Fresh parsley, finely chopped | 10 |
| Fresh chives or green onion, finely chopped | 5 |
| Fine salt | 3 |
| Black pepper | 1 |
| Lemon zest (optional) | 2 |

## Method

1. Soften **100 g butter** until easily mashable (cool room temperature — not melted).
2. Mix in **8 g garlic**, **10 g parsley**, **5 g chives**, **3 g salt**, **1 g pepper**, and optional **2 g lemon zest** until even.
3. Spoon onto parchment, roll into a **3 cm** log, twist the ends. Chill **30–60 minutes** until firm.
4. **For baguettes:** slice the loaf, spread thickly, wrap in foil, warm at **180 °C** for **8–10 minutes**. Or melt slices over hot bread.

## Scale workflow (tare between steps)

```text
Bowl → 100 g soft butter → 8 g garlic → 10 g parsley → 5 g chives
→ 3 g salt → 1 g pepper → (± 2 g zest) → roll → chill
```

## Variations

| Change | Adjustment |
|--------|------------|
| **Roasted garlic** | Replace raw garlic with **20 g** mashed roasted garlic (milder) |
| **Chili** | Add **2 g** chili flakes or **5 g** harissa |
| **Anchovy** | Mash in **15 g** anchovy for steak butter |
| **Honey garlic** | Add **15 g** honey; good on roasted carrots |

## Storage

Fridge **1 week**. Freeze the log up to **2 months**; slice from frozen as needed.
