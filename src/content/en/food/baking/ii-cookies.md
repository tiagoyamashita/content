Chocolate chip cookies
Crisp edges, soft centers, and plenty of chocolate. **All weights are in grams** — zero a bowl on the scale and add each ingredient directly.

**Yield:** about 24 cookies (5 cm / 2 in) · **Oven:** 190 °C (375 °F)

## Equipment

- Kitchen scale (grams)
- Large mixing bowl
- Hand mixer or whisk
- Baking sheet, parchment optional
- Cookie scoop or spoon

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Weight (g) |
|------------|------------|
| All-purpose flour | 250 |
| Unsalted butter, softened | 170 |
| Light brown sugar, packed | 150 |
| Granulated sugar | 100 |
| Large eggs (about 2) | 100 |
| Semi-sweet chocolate chips or chunks | 200 |
| Fine salt | 5 |
| Baking soda | 5 |
| Vanilla extract | 8 |

## Method

1. **Heat oven** to 190 °C. Line baking sheets with parchment.
2. **Dry mix:** In a bowl, combine **250 g flour**, **5 g baking soda**, and **5 g salt**. Whisk 20 seconds.
3. **Cream:** In a large bowl, beat **170 g softened butter**, **150 g brown sugar**, and **100 g granulated sugar** until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
4. **Wet mix:** Beat in **100 g eggs** one at a time, then **8 g vanilla**.
5. **Combine:** Add dry mix to wet. Mix on low or fold until just combined — do not overmix.
6. **Chocolate:** Fold in **200 g chocolate chips**.
7. **Shape:** Scoop **40–45 g** dough per cookie (about 2 tbsp). Space **5 cm** apart on the sheet.
8. **Bake:** **10–12 minutes** until edges are golden and centers look slightly underdone.
9. **Cool:** Rest on the sheet **5 minutes**, then transfer to a rack.

## Scale workflow (tare between steps)

```text
Bowl on scale → tare → 170 g butter → 150 g brown sugar → 100 g granulated sugar → cream
Tare → 100 g eggs → 8 g vanilla → mix
Separate bowl → tare → 250 g flour → 5 g baking soda → 5 g salt → whisk
Fold dry into wet → tare small cup → 200 g chocolate chips → fold in
```

## Variations

| Change | Adjustment |
|--------|------------|
| **Chewier** | Brown sugar **180 g**, granulated **70 g** |
| **Extra chocolate** | Chips **250 g** |
| **Salted** | Flaky salt on top before baking |
| **Oatmeal** | Replace **50 g flour** with **50 g rolled oats** |

## Storage

Room temperature in an airtight container **5 days**. Dough balls freeze well up to **3 months** — bake from frozen, add **2–3 minutes**.
